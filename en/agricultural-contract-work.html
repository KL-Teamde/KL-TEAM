Agricultural Contract Work

The whole year at your side.

Plowing

Efficient due to the latest technology;

Plowing - Cultivating - Turning.

Sowing

With our precision sowing machines and drill combinations,
we are creating the basis for tomorrow's harvest today.

Liquid Fertilization

With our Samson PGII 20 and various attachments,
we bring the nutrients where they belong.

Thanks to the tire pressure control system,
it is of course gentle on the soil and optionally
with the use of a field edge container.

Solid Fertilization

Two Tebbe universal spreaders and a powerful wheel loader
with agricultural tires distribute manure,
sewage sludge or compost quickly and precisely.

Grassland Maintenance

We take care of your grassland maintenance;

Harrowing - Rolling - Reseeding in one operation.

Mowing

No matter whether permanent grassland, arable
grass or GPS - we mow everything!
With the Krone BigM and butterfly combination, impact is guaranteed.

Of course we use an acoustic wildlife rescuer.

Swathing

With a working width of 14m, our shredders
also receive suitable swaths.

Of course, all rotors can be lifted and
moved individually to accommodate
the presses and loading wagons.

Chopping Chain

Thanks to radios on every tractor and customer-oriented employees,
the harvesting of grass and corn runs smoothly!

We are there for you with three forage harvesters.

Pressing

On request, we press chopped and dried
straw into square and round bales with
a diameter of 90 to 150cm.

Trench Maintenance

We can maintain large ditches and receiving waters
with an excavator and mowing basket.

For smaller trenches, however, our trencher is used.
